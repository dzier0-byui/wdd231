Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help golfers find, compare, and choose golf courses in the Salt Lake City area. It will include details like pricing, difficulty levels, features (such as mountain views or driving ranges), and maps for easy navigation.

Scenarios

  • Which public golf courses in Salt Lake are best for beginners?
  • How much does it cost to play 18 holes at Mountain Dell?
  • Are there any scenic golf courses near Salt Lake with mountain views?

Color Scheme

  • #014421 (Dark Green): Used for headings and accents to reflect the color of grass/golf courses.
  • #f4f4f4 (Light Gray): Used for background to keep the design clean and minimal.
  • #ffffff (White): Used for content background areas to create contrast and readability.

Typography

  • Playfair Display: Used for headings to give a classy, professional look.
  • Roboto: Used for body text for readability and simplicity.
